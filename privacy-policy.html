Privacy Policy for Just Music


Last Updated: 16/8/2024
1. Introduction

Welcome to Just Music! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application, Just Music (the "App").

2. Data Collection and Use

Just Music does not collect, store, or process any personal data. Our application simply requests permission to access your device's storage to fetch and display songs that are already saved on your device.

  • File Access Permission: The App requests access to your device's storage to read and retrieve music files. This permission is used solely to provide you with the functionality of browsing and playing your local music files within the App.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not share, sell, or disclose any personal data, as we do not collect any. The App does not interact with or send any data to external servers or third parties.

4. No Data Retention

As we do not collect any personal data, there is no data retention policy applicable. Your music files remain on your device and are not stored or retained by the App.

5. Permissions

  • Storage Access: The App requires permission to access your device's storage to read music files. This permission is necessary for the App to function properly and is not used for any other purpose

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle your data
